# Localized App Store Metadata Matrix

Status: first-pass launch copy. Treat this as the App Store copy source of truth, then run language QA before final upload. Screenshot captions are intentionally short so they can be rendered as overlay text.

Brand policy: keep `Deeper Conversations` as the app name in all locales unless market-specific ASO research recommends a localized title.

Metadata limits to verify in App Store Connect:
- Subtitle: 30 characters.
- Promotional text: 170 characters.
- Description: 4000 characters.
- Keywords: 100 bytes, comma-separated, no competitor names.
- IAP display name: 35 characters.
- IAP description: 55 characters.

## Core English Positioning

App name:
`Deeper Conversations`

Subtitle:
`Questions to bring you closer`

Promotional text:
`Thoughtful prompts for couples, friends, families, and solo reflection. Go beyond small talk with guided sessions and one-time full access.`

Short description:
`Deeper Conversations helps couples, friends, families, and solo reflectors move past small talk with thoughtful prompts, follow-ups, and guided experiences.`

Full description draft:

```text
Go beyond small talk.

Deeper Conversations helps couples, friends, families, and solo reflectors create more meaningful conversations with thoughtful prompts, follow-ups, and guided experiences.

Choose the kind of connection you want:
- Couples, friends, family, or solo reflection
- Light, Honest, and Unfiltered prompts
- Warm Up, Real Talk, and Deep Dive levels
- Follow-ups that help the conversation keep going
- Guided experiences including Fall in Love, Life Story, and Share an Experience

The free version gives you a useful way to start. Full Access unlocks the deeper prompt library, Intimacy prompts, longer sessions, and every guided experience.

One purchase. Lifetime access. No subscription.

Deeper Conversations is not therapy, dating, or a social network. It is a quiet place to ask better questions, listen more closely, and make space for conversations that matter.
```

IAP description:
