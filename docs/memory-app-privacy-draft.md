# Memory App Privacy Statement Draft

Updated: 2026-04-27

## Short Version

Your memory practice data stays on your device.

This app stores scores, progress, and history locally on your device so it can personalize exercises and show your progress over time. That data is not sent to us or shared with third parties.

## Draft Privacy Statement

Memory App is designed to support memory practice in a private, respectful way. Your privacy is simple: your app data stays on your device.

### What is stored on your device

The app may store the following data locally on your device:

- Exercise scores
- Session history
- Progress by memory area
- Strategy lesson progress
- Preferences and accessibility settings
- Reminder preferences
- Optional notes or reflection entries you choose to save

This information is stored locally so the app can personalize your experience, adjust difficulty, and show your progress over time.

### What we collect

We do not collect your memory scores, exercise history, notes, or progress data.

### Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal memory practice data to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties.

### Accounts and sign-in

The app does not require an account to use core features. There is no sign-in required to store your local scores and history.

### Analytics and tracking

The app does not include tracking or analytics frameworks that collect your personal memory practice data.

### Data deletion

Because your app data stays on your device, you can remove it by deleting the app or by using any in-app reset or delete-history controls that are provided.

### Changes in the future

If a future version adds features such as cloud sync, backup, account support, or data sharing, this privacy statement must be updated clearly before those features are introduced.

## In-App Privacy Copy

Suggested short settings copy:

"Your scores and history stay on this device."

Suggested onboarding reassurance:

"Your practice data stays private on your device."

Suggested expanded settings copy:

"This app stores your scores, history, and preferences locally on your device so it can personalize practice and show progress over time. This data is not sent to us."
