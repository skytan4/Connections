Deeper Conversations

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Deeper Conversations is designed to help people have more meaningful conversations. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect your data.

What we collect

Nothing. Deeper Conversations does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

What is stored on your device

The app stores the following data locally on your device to support its features:

  • Your preferences (session defaults, settings)
  • Favorited prompts
  • Progress in guided experiences (Fall in Love, Life Story)
  • Basic usage counts used to determine when to show an optional App Store review prompt

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to us, to Apple, or to any third party.

Accounts and sign-in

Deeper Conversations does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-in, no email collection, and no registration.

Analytics and tracking

Deeper Conversations does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not use cookies, device fingerprints, or identifiers of any kind.

In-app purchases

Deeper Conversations offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock additional features. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-party services

Deeper Conversations does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs beyond Apple's own frameworks (StoreKit for purchases and the native App Store review prompt).

Children's privacy

Deeper Conversations is not directed at children under 13. Some content within the app (such as Intimacy prompts) is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app. No server-side data exists to remove.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Because we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
