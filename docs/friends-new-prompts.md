# New Friends Prompts

Draft additions to bring Friends closer to 10 prompts per tone/topic bucket.

Research anchors used for this pass:

- Adult friendship quality is often described through companionship, help/support, reliable alliance, self-validation, emotional security, and intimacy.
- Good closeness prompts invite gradual self-disclosure, then make room for a responsive answer.
- Strong friendship prompts should include repair, reciprocity, support, boundaries, shared history, and everyday companionship, not only heavy confession.
- Conflict prompts should normalize disagreement while exploring repair, respect, resentment, loyalty, and avoidance.

These are main prompt drafts only. After review, accepted prompts should get follow-ups and production IDs before insertion into `Connections/Data/prompts_en.json`.
